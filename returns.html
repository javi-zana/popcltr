The Promise

Our Promise

If your POP CLTR arrives damaged, defective, or wrong — we'll replace it or refund you in full. No hassle, no runaround.

Under Republic Act No. 7394 (the Consumer Act of the Philippines), we are required by law to honor returns for defective or misrepresented products. We also go beyond what the law requires because we believe in our product — and in you.

Coverage

What We'll Replace or Refund

We draw a clear line. If the problem is on us, we own it. Here's what that looks like.

Yes — We'll Fix It
  • Cans damaged or dented in transit
  • Leaking or defective cans
  • Wrong item or flavor sent
  • Product that arrives expired or past its best-before date
  • Order that never arrived (lost in transit)
What's Not Covered
  • Change of mind after delivery — food safety regulations prevent us from accepting opened beverages
  • Products that were properly delivered but stored improperly
  • Minor cosmetic packaging differences that don't affect the product
  • Delays caused by your courier after we've dispatched

Your Rights

Consumer Rights

Your legal protections as a Filipino consumer are real, and we take them seriously.

Important — RA 7394

Your rights under Republic Act No. 7394 (Consumer Act of the Philippines) are protected. We cannot impose a "No Return, No Exchange" policy — this is prohibited by Philippine law. If you are unable to resolve a concern with us directly, you may contact the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) at dti.gov.ph or visit your nearest DTI office.
