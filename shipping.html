Order Processing

Good to Know

Important Notes

A few things worth knowing before your order arrives.

Key Info
  • POP CLTR is shelf-stable — no refrigeration required during shipping or storage.
  • Delivery runs Monday–Saturday. Standard couriers do not deliver on Sundays.
  • If nobody is home at time of delivery, the courier will attempt re-delivery or leave the package at the nearest hub or branch.
  • Order lost or damaged in transit? Contact us within 3 days of expected delivery at hello@popcltr.com and we'll make it right.
