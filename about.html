About Us

WE'RE
POP CLTR.

A Filipino prebiotic soda brand built for the culture. We refused to accept that a drink could either taste good or be good for you. So we made one that's both.

"Feeling good and having a great time should never be a compromise. That's why we made POP CLTR."

We looked at the soda aisle and saw a gap — drinks full of sugar and nothing else. So we started from the gut and worked out. Apple Cider Vinegar. Inulin Prebiotic. Zero added sugar. Real function, real flavor.

Our Story

BORN FROM A
BETTER QUESTION.

It started with a simple question: why do the drinks we love the most do the least for our bodies?

We grew up drinking soda — cold, fizzy, celebratory. It was part of every meal, every party, every moment. But as we learned more about gut health, we realized the drinks we loved were working against us. Too much sugar. No fiber. No function.

POP CLTR is our answer to that question. A prebiotic soda made with Apple Cider Vinegar and Inulin that does something every time you crack a can. Born in the Philippines, built with real science, and designed to be the drink you actually feel good reaching for.

We believe gut health shouldn't be a chore. It should be a celebration.

91% of Filipinos don't get enough fiber — the gap POP CLTR is built to close
ACV with the "mother" — the real, unfiltered stuff with all its natural goodness
0G added sugar — because you shouldn't have to trade your health for a great drink
What We Stand For

OUR VALUES.

01
Function First

Every ingredient in POP CLTR is there for a reason. Apple Cider Vinegar and Inulin aren't marketing buzzwords — they're clinically recognized functional ingredients with documented effects. We don't add things for the label. We add them because they work.

02
No Compromises

You shouldn't have to choose between a drink that tastes great and one that's good for you. POP CLTR is both — always. The moment we compromise on either, we've missed the point. Great taste AND functional ingredients. Never one without the other.

03
Filipino-Born

We're proud to be a Filipino brand. Our flavors are inspired by local culture — Blueberry Dalandan is just the beginning. We're building something that feels at home here while being built to last on the world stage.

04
Built for the Culture

CLTR is in our name because we're not just selling a drink — we're contributing to a shift in how people think about what they consume. Gut health. Functional food. Making the healthy choice the obvious choice. That's the culture we're building.
