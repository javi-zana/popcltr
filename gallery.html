04 // FULL INGREDIENTS LIST DESCENDING WEIGHT ORDER — RA 10611

Complete Ingredients Declaration

Carbonated Water, Chicory Root Inulin (Prebiotic Fiber), Apple Cider Vinegar (unfiltered), Blueberry Juice Concentrate, Dalandan (Philippine Orange) Juice Concentrate, Erythritol, Monk Fruit Extract (Siraitia grosvenorii), Citric Acid, Natural Blueberry Flavor, Natural Citrus Flavor, Ascorbic Acid (Vitamin C), Sodium Citrate.

ALLERGEN DECLARATION: Contains no known allergens (gluten-free, dairy-free, soy-free, nut-free). Produced in a facility that also processes tree nuts and coconut products. Suitable for vegetarians and vegans.

FUNCTIONAL CLAIM BASIS:
— Chicory Root Inulin: Classified as dietary fiber under FDA Circular 2013-015. A fructooligosaccharide (FOS) that selectively feeds Bifidobacteria and Lactobacillus in the colon. Clinically shown to increase stool frequency, reduce postprandial glucose, and produce short-chain fatty acids (SCFAs) that support mucosal barrier integrity.
— Apple Cider Vinegar (unfiltered, with the mother): Contains acetic acid, polyphenols, and naturally occurring enzymes. Clinical studies indicate ACV reduces postprandial blood glucose spikes (2–6 mmol/L reduction) and supports gastric acid balance. The "mother" provides naturally occurring acetobacter cultures. Classified as a food ingredient — no live culture regulatory approval required.
05 // PHILIPPINE REGULATORY COMPLIANCE FDA PH + TRAIN LAW STATUS

FDA Registration

REQUIRED

Must secure FDA Certificate of Product Registration (CPR) under the Food and Drug Administration Act (RA 9711) before commercial distribution. Registration category: Processed Food — Carbonated Beverage. Expected processing time: 20–40 working days via e-Portal.

Label Compliance

FULLY COMPLIANT

Label design follows FDA Circular 2013-015 (Revised Rules for Food Labeling). All mandatory elements present: product name, net content, ingredients (descending weight), Nutrition Facts panel, manufacturer name and address, lot code, best-before date, country of origin, FDA registration number.

SSB Tax Status

₱0 TAX LIABILITY

Under TRAIN Law (RA 10963), beverages using exclusively non-caloric or natural sweeteners qualify for the SSB tax exemption. POP CLTR uses only monk fruit extract and erythritol as sweeteners — no high-fructose corn syrup, sucrose, or caloric sugar added. Natural juice concentrate sugars are classified as naturally occurring, not added sugars.

Functional / Health Claims

CONDITIONAL

Nutrient function claims ("high fiber," "prebiotic," "source of dietary fiber") are permitted when nutrient levels meet thresholds under RA 10611. POP CLTR's 9g Inulin qualifies for "high fiber" (≥6g per serving). ACV is a standard food ingredient requiring no special live-culture approval — significantly simpler regulatory pathway than probiotic claims. No live culture claim is made.

Packaging Material

FOOD-GRADE COMPLIANT

Aluminum slimline can with food-grade epoxy lining (BPA-NI — non-intent). End material: aluminum alloy. All contact materials must comply with FDA Circular 1993-006 on packaging materials for food. Aluminum is on the approved materials list.

Republic Act 10611

ALIGNED

Philippine Food Safety Act compliance framework: HACCP-aligned production protocol, supplier ingredient certification, microbiological safety testing per Codex Alimentarius standards, and mandatory 2-year shelf-life validation study required for CPR application.
