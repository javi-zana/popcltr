About POP CLTR
What is POP CLTR? +
POP CLTR is a Filipino prebiotic soda made with Apple Cider Vinegar (ACV) and Inulin fiber. It's designed to taste like a great soda while actually supporting your gut health — with zero added sugar. Our first flavor is Blueberry Dalandan, a bold, fizzy combination of blueberry and the Filipino citrus fruit dalandan.
How is POP CLTR different from regular soda? +
Regular sodas are loaded with sugar, artificial flavors, and zero functional benefits. POP CLTR has all the fizz and flavor of a soda, but it's made with Apple Cider Vinegar (with the "mother") and Inulin prebiotic fiber — ingredients that feed your good gut bacteria and support digestion. No added sugar, no crash, just pop.
Is POP CLTR a probiotic drink? +
No — POP CLTR is a prebiotic soda, not a probiotic drink. Probiotics are live bacteria (like those in yogurt or kombucha). Prebiotics are the food that feeds those bacteria. POP CLTR contains Inulin, a soluble fiber from chicory root, which is one of the most well-studied prebiotics available. Think of it as fertilizer for your gut's existing good bacteria.
Where is POP CLTR made? +
POP CLTR is a Filipino brand, formulated and produced in the Philippines. We're proud to be Made in the Philippines 🇵🇭 — from the concept to the can.
Are there more flavors coming? +
Yes! Blueberry Dalandan is our first flavor, but more are in development. Sign up for our newsletter to be first to know when new flavors drop.
Category 02
Ingredients & Gut Health
What's actually in POP CLTR? +
The key functional ingredients are: Apple Cider Vinegar (with the "mother" — the natural prebiotic compounds), Inulin prebiotic fiber (from chicory root), natural Blueberry Dalandan flavoring, and carbonated water. Zero added sugar. The full ingredient list and nutritional information is available on the can.
What does "Apple Cider Vinegar with the mother" mean? +
The "mother" refers to the natural, unfiltered proteins, enzymes, and friendly bacteria found in raw, unprocessed ACV. This is where most of ACV's prebiotic and digestive properties come from. Many commercial ACV products are filtered and pasteurized, removing the mother. POP CLTR uses ACV that retains it.
What is Inulin and what does it do? +
Inulin is a naturally occurring soluble fiber found in chicory root, garlic, onions, and bananas. It passes through your stomach undigested and reaches your colon, where it acts as food (a "prebiotic") for beneficial bacteria like Bifidobacterium and Lactobacillus. These bacteria produce short-chain fatty acids (SCFAs) that support your gut lining, reduce inflammation, and may improve digestion over time. Note: if you're new to inulin, you may experience mild bloating or gas initially — this is normal and usually subsides as your gut adjusts.
Does POP CLTR contain sugar? +
POP CLTR has zero added sugar. Any sweetness you taste comes from natural fruit flavoring. We never use artificial sweeteners, high-fructose corn syrup, or sugar alcohols.
Is POP CLTR safe for people with diabetes? +
POP CLTR contains no added sugar and may be suitable for people managing blood sugar — ACV has been studied for its potential role in supporting blood sugar balance. However, we are not a medical product and cannot provide medical advice. If you have diabetes or a related condition, please consult your doctor or a registered nutritionist before making changes to your diet.
Can I drink POP CLTR if I'm pregnant or breastfeeding? +
POP CLTR is a food beverage made from natural ingredients. However, if you are pregnant or breastfeeding, we recommend consulting your healthcare provider before adding any new food or drink to your routine. This is good practice for any functional food product.
